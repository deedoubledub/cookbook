## Ingredients
- 3/4 cups apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 cup ketchup
- 1 1/2 teaspoons brown sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper

## Directions
1. stir all ingredients in a small bowl until sugar and salt dissolve
