## Ingredients
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 pepper, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 x 8 ounce cans tomato sauce
- 1 can black beans
- 1 can kidney beans
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

## Directions
1. brown ground beef
2. combine all ingredients and cook over medium heat
