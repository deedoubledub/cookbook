## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1/3 cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons minced ginger
- 3 cloves minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- red pepper flakes (to taste)

## Directions
1. mix all ingredients together
2. add to stir fry
