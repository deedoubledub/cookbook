## Ingredients
- 8-9 small apples
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 tablespoons corn starch
- 1 tablespoon flour

## Directions
1. mix it all together and bake in a pie
