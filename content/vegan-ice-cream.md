## Ingredients
- 1 cup cashew milk
- 2 cans coconut milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla bean paste
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

## Directions
1. combine all ingredients
2. chill mixture
3. make ice cream
