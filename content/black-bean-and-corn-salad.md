## Ingredients
- 1 can of whole kernel corn, drained
- 1 can of black beans, drained
- 1/2 red bell pepper, diced
- juice of 1 lime
- 2 tablespoons Salsa
- 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

## Directions
1. mix all ingredients in a large bowl
