## Ingredients
- juice of 1 lime
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 clove of garlic
- 1/4 cup honey
- 1/4 cup hot honey

## Directions
1. combine all ingredients in a saucepan over medium heat

## Use
- brush on chicken during the final phase of cooking
